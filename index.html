One Campus, Endless Possibilities

A cinematic journey through NYU Abu Dhabi

90 seconds • Campus Documentary • 2025

About the Film

This film takes you on a journey through the daily experiences and hidden gems of NYU Abu Dhabi. Starting with an aerial view of campus, we explore meaningful spaces like the Student Affairs hallway, swimming pool, computer labs, and open courtyards.

Each location represents different aspects of campus life - from the charity poster that shows our community's care, to the quiet bird that reminds us to pause and reflect, to students discovering new things in the iMac lab. Together, these moments tell a simple story: every corner of this campus offers a different way to learn, connect, and grow.

Behind the Scenes

Campus Overview

Setting up the drone for the opening aerial view that captures the expansive beauty of NYUAD campus. This shot establishes the calm and open tone of the entire film.

Athletic Excellence

Filming the peaceful moments at the campus pool. The gentle ripples and quiet atmosphere represent the tranquil spaces where students can reflect and recharge.

Aquarium Moments

Capturing the curiosity and wonder represented by the pink fish in the campus aquarium. These small moments of discovery happen everywhere on campus.

Creative Learning

Students in their creative element in the computer lab. This space represents growth, innovation, and the endless possibilities that technology offers for learning.

Campus Life

The heart of campus life where conversations bloom and connections form naturally. Every corner offers opportunities for meaningful interactions and growth.
